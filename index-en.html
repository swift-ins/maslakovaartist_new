MARINA MASLAKOVA

My journey in the beauty world began in 2004. I started out as a salon administrator, and just a year later, I made a bold move — I bought the salon and became its owner. From that moment on, this became more than just work — it became my passion.

I continued to grow, mastering the art of eyelash extensions and later teaching others across Latvia. Every step was filled with love, dedication, and a deep desire to share beauty.

Eventually, life led me to embrace my most important role — becoming a mother. I took a pause in my career to raise my children, but I never lost touch with the beauty industry. I stayed updated, learned new techniques, and when the time was right — I returned with fresh energy and new inspiration.

Today, I continue to do what I love — not only as a lash artist, but also as a certified permanent makeup specialist, trained at the Spanish Academy of Permanent Makeup.

In my work, I use only professional materials from American manufacturers. They help me achieve impeccable quality, enhance natural beauty, and preserve youthfulness. After all, beauty is not about age, but about how we feel and take care of ourselves.

Each client is unique, and I treat everyone with warmth, care, and deep respect. Because true beauty begins with love.
